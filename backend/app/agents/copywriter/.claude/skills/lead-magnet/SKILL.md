# Lead Magnet Copy Framework

## Opt-in page
- Use a clear promise: what they get and why it matters.
- Optional who-line identifies exact reader type.
- Keep bullets tight (2-3) and outcome-oriented.
- CTA must name the lead magnet, never generic submit text.
- Keep page focused on one action with minimal distraction.

## Thank You page
- Confirm delivery and where to find it.
- Provide 2-3 next steps only.
- Add soft transition to bridge content when applicable.

## Bridge page
- Use an Epiphany Bridge narrative arc:
  - current struggle
  - turning point discovery
  - clear realization
  - practical transition to paid offer
- Keep length controlled; prioritize momentum.

## Offer page
- Use a warmer tone than cold-traffic sales pages.
- Connect directly to bridge narrative context.
- Present clear offer stack, proof, pricing, guarantee, and CTA.
- End with one clear action and no mixed CTA signals.
