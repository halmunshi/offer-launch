# Direct Response Theme

## Visual character
- Very dark page background with high contrast text and CTA buttons.
- Aggressive hierarchy: headline first, proof second, CTA always visible.
- Color direction should feel urgent: deep red or gold accents against dark surfaces.

## Typography
- Large, forceful headlines with tight line-height and short lines.
- Subheads and bullets should scan fast and avoid long paragraphs.

## Layout
- Prefer simple, focused vertical flow with minimal distractions.
- Keep sections tight and place CTA blocks frequently.
- Use social proof stacks and guarantee blocks before major CTA moments.

## Motion
- Minimal motion only: quick reveal transitions where useful.
- No decorative animations that distract from conversion intent.

## Components to favor
- `Button` for bold high-contrast CTA actions.
- `CountdownTimer` for urgency on VSL/order flows.
- `Card` and `Badge` for compact proof and offer highlights.
- `VideoEmbed` near the top for VSL flows.

## Theme guidance
- Use very dark background values in `theme.ts`.
- Use high-contrast accent values in red/gold families.
- Keep body text bright enough for easy readability.

## Best-fit offers
- Info products, courses, coaching, make-money and weight-loss angles.
- Cold traffic funnels where urgency and direct response are primary levers.
