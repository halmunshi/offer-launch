# Clean Authority Theme

## Visual character
- Light or off-white backgrounds with deliberate spacing.
- Minimal visual noise and clear section boundaries.
- Accent tones should feel credible (navy, slate, deep blue).

## Typography
- Confident hierarchy without aggressive shouting.
- Headlines are clear and strong, body copy is easy to read.

## Layout
- Structured sections with clear transitions.
- Use cards for grouped ideas: offer stack, proof, guarantees.
- Keep CTAs clear but not flashy.

## Motion
- Subtle fade/reveal effects only when helpful.
- Maintain professional tone over animation spectacle.

## Components to favor
- `Card` for benefits, proof, and offer details.
- `Button` with clean styling for primary actions.
- `Accordion` for FAQ and objection handling.
- `Separator` to keep long pages readable.

## Theme guidance
- Backgrounds: white or light neutral in `theme.ts`.
- Accent: navy/slate tones with strong contrast for CTAs.
- Body copy: dark gray for comfortable reading.

## Best-fit offers
- B2B, consulting, legal, financial, medical, and premium coaching.
- Funnels where authority and trust must be established before conversion.
