# Modern Brand Theme

## Visual character
- Clean layouts with vibrant accent color and rounded UI language.
- Contemporary visual rhythm: mixed columns, cards, and clear spacing.
- Brand feel should support trust and clarity, not hype.

## Typography
- Modern sans-serif hierarchy with strong readability.
- Optional lightweight headline emphasis with restrained flourish.

## Layout
- Alternate between hero, proof, feature cards, and CTA sections.
- Use card-based groupings to break dense content into quick scans.

## Motion
- Subtle, purposeful motion only where it improves comprehension.
- Keep interactions smooth and unobtrusive.

## Components to favor
- `Card`, `Badge`, and `Button` for product-style marketing blocks.
- `TextAnimate` sparingly for one above-the-fold headline reveal.
- `VideoEmbed` when a demo or explainer is central.
- `Tabs` for comparisons or package breakdowns.

## Theme guidance
- In `theme.ts`, use a bright but controlled accent color.
- Keep backgrounds light or softly tinted for readability.
- Prefer `rounded-xl` style radii for modern, approachable surfaces.

## Best-fit offers
- SaaS, ecommerce brands, lifestyle products, and younger-skewed audiences.
- Offers where brand quality and trust matter as much as urgency.
